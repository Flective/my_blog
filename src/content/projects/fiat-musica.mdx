## What It Is

Fiat Musica is a music catalog and commercialization platform built around the artist brand **Broken Light**. The catalog consists of approximately 100–200 original compositions written in the 1980s–90s, preserved on cassette tapes, now being produced professionally using AI production tools with a Human-in-the-Loop methodology.

## The Triangulation Method

Rather than producing a single definitive version of each composition, the methodology produces multiple stylistically distinct versions — what I call a *triangulation suite*. The goal is to reveal the structural integrity of the underlying composition independent of any one production style.

The sonic identity spans two primary registers:

- **80s Power-Pop** — referencing The Cars, Marshall Crenshaw
- **90s Art-Pop** — referencing Talk Talk, Crowded House

## The Backwards Retrospective

The release strategy is structured as a *Backwards Retrospective* — releasing the catalog in reverse chronological order from the production date, which creates a discovery arc rather than an archival dump.
